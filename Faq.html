Q: How do I purchase my order on the website?

1. Click on the product you wish to purchase.

2. Fill in the Purchase Product Form.

3. Choose your payment method.

4. After the steps before this have been done, you need to reconfirm your billing details and agree on the terms and condition.

5. Click on Process Order.
